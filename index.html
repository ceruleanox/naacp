About

The purpose of this day is to provide individuals, non-profits, corporations, clergy, neighborhoods and all who are committed to challenging racism and the structures that support it an opportunity to participate in an act of solidarity through service.

Enrich

Sit in on a sesssion, learn about resources available to you, help out with a meaningful project, and even watch a movie and discuss with other movie watchers afterwards.

Engage

Fill out the census, talk to various community organizations and find out how you can get involved and volunteer, interact with other attendees, and more!

Elevate

Use your talent to support local businesses with their ideas or challenges, offer to connect a fellow attendee with someone in your network, and find ways to lift up one another.



#InServiceWithNAACP

Take our #InServiceWithNAACP Challenge by:

  • Taking a photo or video or writing a tweet or post that shows how you or your family are making a difference
  • Posting the tweet/photo/video to your social media using the #InServiceWithNAACP and inspiring others to take action.
  • Tagging friends and asking them to join the Challenge!
